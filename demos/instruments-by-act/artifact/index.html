Statutory instruments laid before Parliament, by enabling Act

Each line is one Act of Parliament. The y-axis counts how many statutory instruments made under that Act were laid before Parliament in each time bucket. Try removing or adding Acts to compare spikes (e.g. COVID under the Public Health Act 1984) and long tails (e.g. EU exit under the European Union (Withdrawal) Act 2018). This is a self-contained snapshot — the data is baked into the page, no network needed after first load.
